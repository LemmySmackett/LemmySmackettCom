A thirty-something brunette in a fitted navy blazer and cream white pencil skirt storms out of a strip mall Health & Wellness clinic, pursued by a bespectacled man. 

"Linda!" he cries. "Linda, come on!"

"I knew it, I knew it, I knew it." The woman huffs, her black pumps clicking as she strides through the parking lot. "I'm an idiot. I'm—"

"Honey, wait!"

Her heels whirl on the pavement. "Don't honey wait me, Richard! I caught you red handed!"

"Now hey, I wasn't using my hands—"

"Because your head was between her thighs!"

"Linda, dear, I know what it looks like—"

"Her pale, sinuous thighs," she half-sobs.

"—but it wasn't that."

"Oh, really," she snaps back.

"Really!

"That's funny, because it *looked* like you were cheating on your wife by snogging on Ingrid Bergman's Swedish cheesecake!"

"Now, sweetheart, she's my therapist and she has a name."

"Oh, the Scandinavian skank has a name, huh?"

"That's right."

"Does it translate to homewrecking slut?"

"No, that's hemförstörande slampa."

"What?"

"Her name is Ylva Mankentjuv."

"What? Since when do you speak Swedish, Richard??"

"I don't know. You send a man to Ikea enough times, it's bound to happen."

"Tch."

"Hell, we're in there every other week; might as well rent out a showroom."

"Yeah? Well *you* can do that when *you* move out."

"Linda."

"Have fun getting your köttbullar tossed in the cafeteria by Yvonna Humpalot."

"Honey, I'm serious. I wasn't cheating."

"For God's sake, Richard! I saw you!"

"Linda."

 "I saw you! How can you possibly—"

"It was therapy."

"T-therapy?!"

"For my sensory issue."

"Of all the—"

"Every day is a struggle for me, Linda. You know how it's been a struggle."

"Oh, it's gonna *be* a struggle when we get in that courtroom—"

"I've got ADHD, ARFID, SIDS—"

"You can't have SIDS, Richard!" Linda stomps her foot. "SIDS is for dead babies!"

"Well, who's to say."

"And you know what? I thank God you've only shot blanks for the past five years, because if I was pregnant right now, I'd pray for a miscarriage!"

"That's hurtful, Linda."

"I'd sell my soul to satan for it!"

"Real hurtful. But I'm going to let it go and be the bigger man, because I know you're only doing it out of anger."

Her eyes narrow. "You haven't seen what I can do out of anger."

"Honey-buns."

"Don't."

"Sugar roll."

She turns away. "Stop it."

"You're the one who suggested therapy in the first place."

She spins back. "A couples counselor!"

"I know."

"I wanted us to work on our marriage, Richard! Together!" Her hands fly into the air. "Not have you go off and hump some Nordic Ace of Bass slag!" She bows her head, clamping her hands tight over her eyes. "Jesus, God, why?"

"Linda..."

"Why does she have to be so blonde??"

Richard steps forward.

"Fucking platinum!"

He takes her hands in his. "Sweetbeandream, listen."

Her eyes stay down.

"Everything's my fault. It's always been my fault."

"It has." She sniffs.

"Therapy has shown me that I haven't been the man you deserve. I've been difficult, and a burden, and worst of all, I haven't been there for you. Intimately. Spiritually." He caresses her cheek. "Emotionally."

She glances up. "You ruined my life."

"I did," he whispers. "But now everything is going to be okay."

"No. It'll never be okay." She gulps and shakes her head. "Nothing will ever be okay." 

"No, no, it will because—Linda, look at me."

"I can't."

"Linda, please."

She looks again, her eyes brimming with tears. "What, Richard?"

"It's going to be okay," he says in that gentle, steady voice that always puts her heart at ease. "Because I can finally eat you out."

She blinks and croaks. "What?"

"My sensory issue." He beams. "I finally beat it!" 

"Sensory issue?" She numbly repeats. "What does that matter?" She asks, not wanting the answer.

"Well, I've always struggled with cunnilingus and Ylva helped me realize it's my ARFID. Ya know, from my autism."

As her stomach drops and the world lurches, Linda balls her fists to keep her hands from trembling. "Richard..." It takes all her strength to remain on her feet.

Richard rambles on. "And I just had to familiarize my palette with the taste and texture, a light touch on the lips here, a quick lick there, standard ARFID treatment really—"

"Just stop..talking..." She mutters, her nausea giving way to a cold sweat and the heady rush of a adrenaline. "..please..."

"And the funniest thing? This whole time, the whole time, I thought it was a texture issue, like how I can't eat cruciferous vegetables, right? But actually, it turns out I struggle with piquant flavors, and, lucky me, the Swedish have the most neutral, PH balanced vaginal environments of any women on the planet. Actually, it's not too different from the köttbullar at IKE—"

Richard's glasses sail over the hood of a Volvo XC90 as Linda's fist shatters his jaw. "SHUT UP!"
