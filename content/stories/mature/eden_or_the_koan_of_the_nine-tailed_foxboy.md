"What?"

"Okay."

"Lupercalia, on the other hand, was in February, like the Chinese New Year, and featured a wild, wolf-themed feast with fertility rites that involved citizens running buck naked through the streets while lashing each other with strips of hide and leather—"

"Which is what we'll be doing in the Rotunda at four."

"What??"

"Oh. Yeah, they're allowed to do that now."

"What?"

"Congress just passed a new law."

"New law?"

"Yep. It's called E.D.E.N—the Efficient Driverless Exemption Network. It even came with its own tagline."

"Laws have taglines now?"

"In America, the road is paradise."

"You're taking the piss."

"No, but if you go to the *West* Atrium—"

"Just tell me about the law."

"So you know how AI-controlled cars have all these advanced sensors and, like, superhuman reaction times?"

"Sure."

"Well, independent researchers found that while obeying the rules of the road, AI-controlled cars only get into 2.10 accidents per one million miles versus a human at 4.68 accidents for the same. That's a 55% reduction."

"Yeah, when they're obeying the rules of the road."

"Exactly, and that's the kicker."

"Eh?"

"Those same researchers were able to demonstrate a whopping 91% reduction in accidents if they just let the AI drive the car however it pleased."

"What?"

"The researchers scientifically proved that traffic laws designed for humans actually made travel *less* safe for AI-controlled cars."

"I don't buy that—"

"And given the millions of projected lives saved, Congress simply had to act."

"Uh-huh. And I'm sure the fact that over half of the Senate and two-thirds of the House are already AI had no bearing on that decision."

"What can I say? Democracy has spoken and the American people have chosen to be ruled and represented by machines of loving grace."

"So as a feeble human, I gotta sit at this red while some clanker Corolla can do donuts in the box?"

"Yes, but remember, the AI would only ever do donuts in the box *if" it determined that said donuts made travel safer."

"Unbelievable."

"I'm surprised you didn't hear about this, man. It was all over CNN."

"I don't watch the news."

"Me either. I follow a guy on X called RetardRepoast for insightful political analysis and Nikita Boar memes."

"Look, everything's AI-generated from everywhere, real or fake, so who can tell anything from anything these days?"

"Truke, but surely you noticed more cars driving erratically?"

"For the DFW metroplex? Not really."

"Fair."

"You realize this law makes every human a second-class citizen on the road, right?"

"Speak for yourself. This bear is in exile."

"Eh?"

"Suspended license, remember?"

"Same reason I got divorced and was banned from all the ride-share apps."

"Yiffing."
